# Plan lifecycle

## Active
Work in `plans/active/<date>-<slug>/`.

## Archive
Once the work ships or is abandoned, move the folder to `plans/archive/`.

## What lives in a good initiative folder

- spec
- architecture
- NFRs
- decision matrix or ADR
- plan
- tasks
- rollout and observability notes
- reports and research
- incident or follow-up notes when relevant

## Rule

Artifacts are part of the engineering output, not disposable scratch notes.
